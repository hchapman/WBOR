DJ of the Week: 11-13-2013:

Emily Hochman and Kelsey Berger

Kid Sister Radio


Give a Brief description of your show: names, dj names, how long have you been doing this, style, etc.
We are Emily Hochman and Kelsey Berger. We’re juniors and we host Kid Sister Radio every single week. I’m new to the industry, but I’ve been a fan of DJ EMiHooch since the beginning of her career in sophomore year. We compile music independently before the show, and we play our songs popcorn style.

Song that makes you cry?
“Rainbow Connection” by Kermit the Frog.

Most amazing concert experience?
Jeff Mangum at the State Theater – I’m taking Emily’s word for it, I missed this concert and was too lazy to get my refund.

Best song to study to?
Emily studies to Kid Koala because it rocks and there are no confusing words to interfere with her reading.

If you were stuck on a desert island, what three albums would you take with you and why?
I’d bring three Radiohead albums, because then Emily would kill herself and I’d get more rations #winning
I also just really like Radiohead.

If you could meet any musician, dead or alive, who would it be and why?
Emily wants to meet Edith Piaf, and I want to meet Gene Kelley (triple threat!).

Most played song on your iTunes?
“Food Is Still Hot” – Karen O & the Kids.

Favorite era of music?
Emily votes for the 60s – mainly for the girl groups. I vote for the musicians of the 90s, because they were down with denim on top and so am I.

Favorite place on campus?
That bench between Belmont St and Thorne (me), or the walkway between Druck and Hatch (Emily). We’re very into transitional spaces.

Favorite vegetable?
Sweet taters, because they’re sweet and they’re carbs!

When can we tune into your show?
Saturday, 2pm-3pm!
