DJ of the Week, 02-05-2014:

Janki & Megan

Pillow Talk


Give a Brief description of your show: names, dj names, how long have you been doing this, style, etc.
Names: Janki Kaneria and Megan Massa, Class of 2014
DJ Names: Disk Janki and Megan
Show: Pillow Talk
Duration: Four years strong, baby!

Greatest musician of all time and why?
Janki: I plead the fifth
Megan: Bruce, no explanation necessary (says the Jersey girl)

Song that makes you cry?
Janki: “You Raise Me Up” by Josh Groban
Megan: “Long Live the Queen” by Frank Turner

Most amazing concert experience?
Janki: Concerts just aren’t my thing. I prefer listening to music in my car with the windows down.
Megan: *gives Janki a sidelong glance* That time when I was front row at a Gaslight Anthem concert and Brian Fallon total gave me the head-nod. *Janki shakes her head*

Best song to drive to?
Janki: Any song by the Civil Wars
Megan: I can’t listen to music when I study…I’d just sing along

If you were stuck on a desert island, what three albums would you take with you and why?
Janki: Let’s Be Still by The Head and Heart to put me in a good mood; I Am Sasha Fierce by Beyoncé because no explanation needed; and The Little Mermaid Soundtrack to relive my childhood.
Megan: Handwritten by The Gaslight Anthem because I never get tired of listening to them; Beyoncé by Beyoncé because everyone needs a little fierce in their life; and RENT the Original Broadway Cast Recording because it’s awesome and goes on forever.

Best guilty pleasure and/or sing-a-long song and why?
Janki: “Timber” by Pitbull ft. Kei$ha
Megan: “Down” by Jay Sean ft. Lil Wayne

If you could meet any musician, dead or alive, who would it be and why?
Janki: Sonu Nigam because I love him. I love him. I just love him.
Megan: Patti Smith because she’s do dope.

Most played song on your iTunes?
Janki: “A Thousand Miles” by Vanessa Carleton. LOLOLOL middle school.
Megan: “Mae” by The Gaslight Anthem. Surprise surprise.

Favorite era of music?
Janki: 80’s pop
Megan: 80’s punk rock

Favorite place on campus?
Janki: 80’s punk rock
Megan: Third floor of Kanbar!

Favorite vegetable?
Janki: Kale right now, but it changes monthly
Megan: Marinara sauce….that’s a vegetable, right? And it’s pronounced ‘madanad.’ Don’t believe me? See here: http://youtu.be/qcp8rN-YqLw.

When can we tune into your show?
Sundays from 10-11am!
